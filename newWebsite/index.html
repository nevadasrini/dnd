So, what is D&D?

The Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game is about storytelling in worlds of swords and sorcery. It shares elements with childhood games of make-believe. Like those games, D&D is driven by imagination. LET'S GET STARTED!

HOW DO YOU PLAY?

D&D is very complex and has many exceptions and intricacies, which makes it hard to thoroughly explain to a beginner. However, it's not necessary to know all the rules before your first campaign, so don't worry about knowing all the rules exactly. It's easier to figure it out as you play, and you'll get much better as you gain experience. For now, all you need to do is create a character that you'd like to roleplay and follow your DM's instructions. However, you can still check out the official player's handbook if you'd like to know more.

WHAT WILL I NEED?

Not much at all! What you'll need to bring depends on what kind of campaign you'll be playing and how your DM operates, but here are the basics:
- The player's handbook
- A set of dice
- Character sheets
- A pen or pencil
- Snacks!
If you are the DM, you'll need to bring a map, markers to draw with, and a prepared story for your campaign.
